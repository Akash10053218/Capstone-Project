Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

You can report bugs through our contact form or by emailing support@spacequest.com. Please include as much detail as possible about the issue.

Is the game free to play?

Yes! Do or Die is free to play. We offer optional cosmetic purchases that don't affect gameplay.

Can I play with friends?

Absolutely! You can create private lobbies and invite friends, or join public matches together.

What platforms are supported?

Do or Die is available on Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android with cross-platform play support.

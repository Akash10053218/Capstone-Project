About the Game

Discover what makes Do or Die! unique

"Do or Die!" is an innovative social deduction game developed as a college project, inspired by the popular Among Us. The game challenges players to complete tasks while a secret saboteur tries to ruin everything. The only way to survive is to stay alert, communicate effectively, and trust no one!

Set in a futuristic space station, players must work together to complete various tasks and maintain the station's systems. However, one or more players are secretly saboteurs whose goal is to eliminate the crew without being discovered. The game combines strategy, deception, and quick thinking in an intense multiplayer experience.

How to Play

Master the game in three simple steps

01

Join a Match

Create or join a game room with 4-10 players. Wait for the match to begin and discover your role - Crew Member or Saboteur.

02

Complete Objectives

Crew members must complete tasks around the map while saboteurs try to eliminate them or sabotage the mission. Use your wits to survive!

03

Win the Game

Crew wins by completing all tasks or identifying all saboteurs. Saboteurs win by eliminating enough crew members or preventing task completion.

Welcome to Do or Die!

A fast-paced social deduction game where every move matters. Work together or betray your team – the choice is yours.

About the Game

"Do or Die!" is a college project inspired by Among Us. It challenges players to complete tasks while a secret saboteur tries to ruin everything. The only way to survive is to stay alert and trust no one!

Game Features

  • 4–10 Players per match
  • Hidden Roles: Crew vs. Saboteur
  • Randomized Maps every game
  • Runs smoothly on basic PCs

About Space Quest

Discover the story behind the galaxy's most thrilling adventure

The Game

Space Quest is an immersive multiplayer adventure game set in the depths of space. Work together with your crewmates to complete critical missions, or play as the mysterious infiltrator and sabotage the mission from within.

With stunning visuals, engaging gameplay, and endless possibilities, Space Quest offers an experience like no other. Every game is different, every decision matters, and every player could be the key to victory or defeat.

The game combines elements of strategy, deception, and teamwork in a unique space setting. Players must use their wits, observation skills, and communication abilities to succeed in this thrilling adventure.

The Story

In the year 2157, humanity has expanded across the galaxy, establishing space stations and colonies on distant planets. However, not all is peaceful in the cosmos. A mysterious organization has infiltrated various space missions, threatening the safety of crews across the galaxy.

As a crew member aboard a space station, your mission is to complete tasks and ensure the safety of your team. But beware - among your crewmates, there may be impostors working to sabotage your mission. Trust no one, question everything, and work together to survive.

Every mission is a test of your ability to work as a team, identify threats, and complete objectives under pressure. Will you be the hero who saves the day, or will you be the one who brings the mission to ruin?

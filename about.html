About the Game

What is DO or DIE!?

DO or DIE! is a bluffing and survival-based multiplayer game that pits players against each other in a battle of wits, deception, and strategy. Each player is assigned a secret role with unique objectives, creating an atmosphere of suspicion and intrigue.

Can you trust your teammates? Or are they working against you? In DO or DIE!, nothing is as it seems, and survival depends on your ability to read others while maintaining your own facade.

Game Features

  • Multiple Roles: Play as Survivor, Saboteur, or Detective, each with unique abilities and goals
  • Task System: Complete objectives to progress or sabotage them to hinder others
  • Voting Mechanics: Discuss, debate, and vote to eliminate suspicious players
  • Real-time Multiplayer: Connect with players worldwide in fast-paced matches
  • Strategic Depth: Every decision matters in this game of psychological warfare

The Experience

DO or DIE! combines elements of social deduction, strategy, and survival games into a unique experience. Whether you're working together to complete tasks or secretly sabotaging the mission, every match is different and full of tension.

Communication is key, but so is deception. Master the art of reading body language, analyzing behavior, and maintaining your cover to emerge victorious.

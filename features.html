Game Features

Discover what makes Do or Die an unforgettable gaming experience

Multiplayer Action

Play with up to 10 players in thrilling multiplayer sessions. Team up or face off in epic space adventures. Cross-platform support ensures you can play with friends on any device.

Strategic Gameplay

Use your wits and strategy to complete objectives or deceive your opponents. Every move counts! Analyze situations, make quick decisions, and outsmart your enemies.

Multiple Maps

Explore diverse space stations and planets, each with unique challenges and secrets to discover. From abandoned research facilities to bustling space ports.

Customization

Personalize your character with hundreds of skins, hats, and accessories to stand out from the crowd. Express your unique style in the galaxy.

Achievements

Unlock achievements, climb leaderboards, and prove your skills in competitive ranked matches. Track your progress and compete with players worldwide.

Social Features

Chat with friends, form squads, and build lasting friendships in the Do or Die community. Join clans and participate in community events.

Multiple Game Modes

Experience various game modes including Classic, Hide & Seek, Proximity Chat, and Custom modes. Each mode offers a unique gameplay experience.

Regular Updates

Enjoy regular content updates with new maps, features, and improvements. Our development team is constantly working to enhance your gaming experience.

Global Servers

Play on servers located around the world for the best connection and lowest latency. Find matches quickly and play smoothly with players globally.

Characters / Roles

Each player is randomly assigned one of three roles at the start of each match. Your role determines your objectives and how you should play.

TEAM: SURVIVORS

Survivor

The Survivor's goal is simple: complete all tasks and identify the Saboteurs before they eliminate everyone.

Abilities:

  • Complete tasks to progress the mission
  • Vote to eliminate suspicious players
  • Report dead bodies to call emergency meetings
  • Use security cameras to monitor areas

Win Condition: Complete all tasks OR eliminate all Saboteurs

TEAM: SABOTEURS

Saboteur

The Saboteur's mission is to eliminate Survivors and prevent task completion without being discovered.

Abilities:

  • Sabotage tasks and systems
  • Eliminate Survivors when alone
  • Vent through the map quickly
  • See other Saboteurs (but can't communicate)

Win Condition: Eliminate Survivors until numbers are equal OR prevent task completion

SPECIAL ROLE

Detective

The Detective is a special Survivor role with enhanced investigation abilities to find the truth.

Abilities:

  • All Survivor abilities
  • Investigate players to gather clues
  • Access restricted information
  • Reveal one player's role per game

Win Condition: Same as Survivor, but with investigative advantage

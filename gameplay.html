How to Play

Master the art of space survival and deception

Getting Started

1

Join a Game

Start by joining a public lobby or create your own private game with friends. You can play with 4-10 players in each match.

2

Choose Your Role

You'll be randomly assigned as either a Crewmate or an Impostor. Crewmates must complete tasks, while Impostors must eliminate the crew.

3

Complete Objectives

As a Crewmate, complete all tasks to win. As an Impostor, eliminate crewmates or sabotage the mission to claim victory.

Roles & Objectives

Crewmate

  • Complete all assigned tasks
  • Report dead bodies
  • Participate in emergency meetings
  • Identify and vote out Impostors

Impostor

  • Eliminate Crewmates
  • Sabotage systems
  • Use vents to move quickly
  • Blend in and avoid suspicion

Pro Tips

For Crewmates

  • Always complete tasks in groups when possible
  • Pay attention to player movements
  • Use security cameras to monitor activity
  • Don't trust anyone blindly

For Impostors

  • Create alibis by appearing to do tasks
  • Use sabotage strategically
  • Target isolated players
  • Blame others during meetings

Game Mechanics

Tasks

Crewmates must complete various tasks around the map. Tasks include fixing wiring, downloading data, and more.

Emergency Meetings

Players can call emergency meetings or report dead bodies to discuss and vote on who to eliminate.

Sabotage

Impostors can sabotage systems like lights, oxygen, or communications to create chaos.

Voting

During meetings, players discuss and vote to eliminate suspected Impostors. Majority vote wins.
